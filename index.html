About the project

Built for early detection

OncosenseAI is a research-grade clinical AI interface designed to support early detection of gastrointestinal cancers — pancreatic, gastric, and oesophageal — where early-stage identification can multiply survival outcomes by nearly 9×.

The pipeline integrates patient symptom scoring, laboratory biomarkers, genomic profiling, and validated survival models into a cohesive 8-stage clinical decision support tool. Treatment recommendations are anchored to ESMO 2023, NCCN v2.2024, and NICE NG12/NG83 guidelines.

Validated retrospectively against 500 SEER patients across three cancer sites, with full TRIPOD reporting compliance and explainability through SHAP feature importance analysis.
