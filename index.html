The Platform

One platform.
Two entry points.

OncosenseAI is designed for both sides of the diagnostic journey — meeting patients where they are, and equipping clinicians with the tools they need.

🧑
For Patients
Self-triage, early awareness, and cost-conscious guidance — from any device, no medical background needed.
💬
Interactive Symptom Triage
Conversational UI built on NICE NG12 standards. Flags alarm features such as jaundice, unexplained weight loss, and dysphagia.
👁️
Visual Biomarker Assessment
Upload-based image analysis for visible clinical signs. OpenCV-powered framework with guided prompts for scleral and skin changes.
📋
Cost-Effective Next Steps
Prioritised, tiered recommendations to avoid unnecessary expense while ensuring urgent cases reach care fastest.
🩺
For Clinicians
SEER-validated risk scores, automated referral documents, and treatment matching at point-of-care.
📊
Validated Risk Scoring
Logistic Regression model (AUROC 0.79) with SHAP explainability — showing which features drove each patient's risk tier.
📄
Automated Referral Packets
One-click PDF generation with risk tier, alarm features, composite score, and NICE NG12 justification for 2WW referrals.
🔬
Treatment Matching
NCCN/ESMO/NICE-aligned recommendations with live ClinicalTrials.gov integration and genomic marker cross-referencing.
Data-Backed Trust

Built on real population data

Every metric comes from 5-fold cross-validated models trained on genuine SEER registry variables — not simulated or prevalence-derived features.

Equity Finding
Analysis revealed a 2-month survival gap — Black patients (median 7mo) vs White patients (median 9mo). OncosenseAI is designed specifically to surface and address this disparity in primary care decision-making.

Pipeline Architecture

Four modules. One pipeline.

Each module is sequentially integrated — deterministic guideline logic feeding ML inference, feeding visual diagnostics, treatment matching, and automated reporting.

MODULE 01
🧠
Symptom Intelligence Engine
Three-tier NICE NG12-aligned risk stratification. Dual-layer: deterministic alarm logic + ensemble ML composite scoring across 19 clinical features.

🔭
