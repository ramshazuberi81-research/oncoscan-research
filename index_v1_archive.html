OncosenseAI: An Integrated Clinical AI Pipeline for Early Detection of Abdominal Cancers in Primary Care Settings

A four-module end-to-end clinical AI system validated on SEER Cancer Registry data (n=500). Prospective IRB cohort (n=100) in preparation for full validation.

2026
DATASET VALIDATED

SEER Registry Validation Study: 12-Month Mortality Prediction in Abdominal Cancers Using Ensemble ML

Zuberi R.

Retrospective validation on 500 SEER Cancer Registry patients. Pancreas (n=264), stomach (n=146), oesophagus (n=90). 5-fold cross-validated ensemble achieves AUROC 0.753 with NPV 0.853 at 95% sensitivity. Survival analysis demonstrates 8.8× median survival advantage for localised vs distant stomach cancer.

Code → Paper pending
